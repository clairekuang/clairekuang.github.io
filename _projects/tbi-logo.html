1.02The Ring and the Wrap

PLACEHOLDER TEXT: Avoiding the most commonly associated symbol of boxing - the boxing glove, and the typical aggressive colors of competitive gyms (violent red, black, blue). Instead, focused on the dualisms of martial arts: precision, technique, and science of fighting represented through an axonometric, dissected boxing ring. Ritual, rhythm, and flow represented by the handwrap. Muted solids and vared color scheme with captures the makeshift do-it-yourself attitude while remaining grounded and professional.
